About Badgers Inn

Nestled in the beautiful South Downs National Park, Badgers Inn is a historic country inn just 5 minutes from the village of Petworth. The inn features beautifully decorated rooms with en-suite bathrooms, and serves outstanding food made from fresh, local produce.

We'll spend our days taking lovely walks around the South Downs, exploring the stunning countryside, and enjoying the peace and quiet of this beautiful area.
