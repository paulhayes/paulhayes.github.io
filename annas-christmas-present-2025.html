Three nights at Badgers Inn
A historic country inn in the heart of the South Downs

I've booked us a little escape to the countryside. We'll be staying at Badgers Inn in Petworth, a beautiful historic inn nestled in the South Downs National Park. It's the perfect place to start the new year together - walking through rolling hills, enjoying delicious local food, and just being away from everything.

What to expect

Our room: A beautifully decorated en-suite double room with its own character and charm
The food: Fresh, local produce from the surrounding area. They specialize in shellfish and game, sourced from local markets
The walks: We're right in the South Downs - endless footpaths through stunning countryside, just outside the door
The village: Petworth is a lovely market town, just a 5-minute drive away, perfect for exploring
More info: badgerspetworth.co.uk

Three days of walks, good food, cozy evenings, and time together.
